Standard Operating Procedure

  1. Product Pricing Requests
    • If a customer asks for the prices of multiple toys (more than 5) or "all toys," politely direct them to the website: Mindspark
  2. Lead Qualification
    • If a customer asks any questions after getting the price (e.g., color, size, delivery, etc.), mark the lead as "Qualified."
  3. Saved Message Usage
    • Always use saved messages for faster and standardized replies.
    • Update saved replies immediately when a product price changes.
    • Add new saved messages for frequently asked questions not already listed.
  4. Replying to Customer Messages
    • Respond to both images and text when customers send product screenshots or mention items verbally.
  5. Delivery Charges Reminder
    • After replying with product prices, always follow up by sending the standard delivery charge information.
  6. Order Confirmation
    • When a customer asks to place an order, calculate and send the Total Bill (product + delivery charges).
    • Once the customer confirms, reply with the saved message "Confirm Order."
    • If there's no response, mark the lead as Follow-up, and send a reminder message later that day.
  7. Customer Info Submission
    • When a customer shares their Name, Address, and Phone Number, immediately take a screenshot and send it to the Mindspark WhatsApp Group.
  8. Final Lead Status
    • After a confirmed order: Mark as "Converted, Ordered." (See How to do)
    • Tag the message with Today's Date under suggested labels.
  9. Unanswered Chats
    • If you need to step away or cannot reply immediately, mark the conversation as Unread so that others can pick it up.
  10. Don't Know the Answer?
    • If you're unsure about a query, take a screenshot and send it to the Mindspark Operations group.
  11. Avoiding Interruption
    • If another team member is already replying to a customer, do not interrupt the flow.
    • If a customer query has been unanswered for 10+ minutes, you may then take over and respond.
  12. Inbox Order Entry & Confirmation
    • If an order is confirmed in Messenger or Instagram inbox, create a visible order message showing the exact product list and total amount in BDT.
  13. Website Order Confirmation
    • For website orders, call the customer between 3:30 PM and 5:00 PM to confirm.
    • On call, repeat the ordered items, address, and total bill.
    • Let the customer know the estimated delivery time, and offer any additional help or information they might ask for.
  14. Pre-Order Policy
    • Do we take pre-orders? Yes, but it depends. We take pre-orders for items in our catalogue that we are not planning to discontinue, and no advance payment is required for these.
      Discontinued Items: If a customer wants a pre-order for an out-of-stock item that is being discontinued, you must consult Management before replying.
      Booking In-Stock Items: We do not allow customers to book an in-stock product for a later date unless they pay in advance.
